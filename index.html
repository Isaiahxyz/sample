5 Characters from Noli Me Tangere
  • Padre Damaso
    • A very brash and arrogant friar that acted superior to others.
  • Maria Clara
    • The fiance of Juan Crisostomo.
  • Capitan Tiago
    • An influential businessman in San Diego and the father of Maria Clara
  • Crisostomo Ibarra
    • Theorized as the stand-in for Rizal
  • Teniente Guevarra
    • An elderly lieutenant of the Guardia Civil and a close friend of Don Rafael
